# StudySwap AI Roadmap (Sage the Raccoon)

This document tracks high-priority AI features to be implemented as the platform grows.

## 🚀 Priority AI Enhancements

### 1. Sage Deep-Scan (Collective Intelligence Synthesis)
*   **Concept**: A "Deep Research" mode for course-wide context.
*   **Functionality**: Sage scans the *entire* library of a specific course (all uploaded PDFs, slides, and notes) to answer questions.
*   **User Value**: Instead of chatting with one file, students get a synthesized answer based on the collective knowledge of their entire class.
*   **Example**: "What did the professor say about the midterm?" -> Sage scans 20 student uploads to find common themes and specific mentions.

### 2. Sage Source-Grounding (Anti-Hallucination Layer)
*   **Concept**: Visual verification of AI claims.
*   **Functionality**: Every response from Sage includes "mini-thumbnails" or direct links to the exact page/slide in a classmate's shared document that supports the claim.
*   **User Value**: Builds trust in the shared materials and the AI. Students can instantly verify Sage's explanations by looking at the original source material.

---

## 💡 Other Potential Ideas
*   **Sage Note-Gap Detective**: Proactively identifies missing course materials and incentivizes uploads.
*   **Sage Multi-Modal Transcriber**: Specialized OCR for messy student handwriting and whiteboard photos.
*   **Sage Semantic Connector**: Matches students' specific struggles with the best-suited shared material in the database.
